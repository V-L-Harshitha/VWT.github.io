Vangipuram Welfare Trust

Comitted to serve!!!
















Objectives


1. To aid poor meritorious students by providing schlorships.
2. impart all aspects of education based on the modern and practical concepts.
3. To construct and aid for running schools, colleges, and educational institutions for the benefit of the students at Vangipuram and surrounding villages in Guntur Dist. or at any place of the choice of trustees of the trust for the benefit of the students.
4. To provide grants or donations for reconstruction of the Agasteswara Swamy temple and other temples
5. To provide required basic amenities to the village and to improve the standards of living.
6. To print, publish and to distribute to assist or promote the objects of the Trust.
7. To arrange and organize lectures, debates, discussions, study tours, conferences, or radio-talks
8. Raise funds through donations and contributions to support all the above activities



About Us



Our History



The members of the Trust are hailed or associated with the village Vangipuram through their ancestors and now settled world wide.We decided to do some philonthropic activities which will be useful for the society and in this connection the name vangipuram is given for the purpose of recognition only
